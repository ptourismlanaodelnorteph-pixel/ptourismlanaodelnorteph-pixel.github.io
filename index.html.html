Discover the stories, culture, and heritage that shape the LaNorte Province. From ancient artifacts to modern art, our exhibits bring history to life for all ages. Visit us and explore the heart of our community’s past and present.

The LaNorte Provincial Museum was founded in 1978 to preserve and celebrate the unique heritage of the LaNorte region. Our mission is to educate, inspire, and connect visitors through engaging exhibits, cultural programs, and community partnerships. With a growing collection of over 100 artifacts, the museum highlights local history, Indigenous traditions, natural wonders, and the creative spirit of LaNorte’s people.
